## üí° ATL/BTL Ratio Analysis

### Industry Standard Discovered

Professional budgets follow a remarkably consistent pattern:

```
ATL (Above-the-Line):    ~20% of total budget
BTL (Below-the-Line):    ~80% of total budget

Standard Ratio: 1:4.03
```

This means **for every $1 spent on talent/creatives, $4.03 goes to crew/production**.

## üé¨ Professional Budget Patterns Discovered

### Pattern 1: Amortization vs Pattern Budgets

**Amortization Budget:**
- Shows ALL series costs (prep + wrap + all episodes)
- Example: $3.9M √∑ 12 episodes = $325K per episode
- Used for: Series financing, network deals

**Pattern Budget:**
- Shows SINGLE typical episode cost
- Includes amortization line item (allocated share)
- Example: $1.84M per episode
- Used for: Episode tracking, variance analysis

**System Implication:** Need toggle between "Series Total" and "Per Episode" views

---

### Pattern 2: Multi-Period Calculation Formula

**Every line item uses:**
```
Amount √ó Units √ó Hours √ó Rate = Subtotal

Example - Script Coordinator:
  Prep:    40 days √ó 14 hrs √ó $26.15 = $14,644
  Shoot:   60 days √ó 14 hrs √ó $26.15 = $21,966
  Hiatus:  20 days √ó 14 hrs √ó $26.15 = $7,322
  Wrap:    10 days √ó 14 hrs √ó $26.15 = $3,661
  Total: $47,593
```

---

### Pattern 3: Platform-Specific Rate Tiers

**Same Union, Different Rates:**
- **Network/Broadcast:** Highest rates (standard DGA/IATSE)
- **Cable:** 15-25% lower (DGA Sideletter rates)
- **SVOD/Streaming:** Varies by platform and budget tier
- **Low Budget:** Special agreements (<$2.6M)

**Evidence from Data:**
- Cable series: $5.6M (9 eps) = $622K/ep
- Network pilot: $6.5M (1 ep)
- Cable uses lower ATL% (observed 3.7% in one budget)

---

### Pattern 4: Location Iteration Process

**Discovered:** Budgets go through 1-6 versions per location

Example - Atlanta:
- Ver 01: Initial estimate
- Ver 02: +$8,500 (crew adjustments)
- Ver 03: +$2,200 (fringe corrections)
- Ver 04-06: Minor tweaks (<$5K changes)

**Insight:** Professional budgeting is iterative, not one-shot

**AI Feature:** Version control and change tracking built-in
