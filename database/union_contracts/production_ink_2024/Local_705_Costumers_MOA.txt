July 17, 2024
LOCAL MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT OF AUGUST 1, 2024
BETWEEN ALLIANCE OF MOTION PICTURE AND TELEVISION PRODUCERS
AND INTERNATIONAL ALLIANCE OF THEATRICAL STAGE EMPLOYEES AND
MOVING PICTURE TECHNICIANS, ARTISTS AND ALLIED CRAFTS OF THE
UNITED STATES, ITS TERRITORIES AND CANADA
AND LOCAL 705 THEREOF
THIS LOCAL MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT IS BETWEEN THE INTERNATIONAL
ALLIANCE OF THEATRICAL STAGE EMPLOYEES AND MOVING PICTURE
TECHNICIANS, ARTISTS AND ALLIED CRAFTS OF THE UNITED STATES, ITS
TERRITORIES AND CANADA AND MOTION PICTURE COSTUMERS, LOCAL 705,
I.A.T.S.E., ON THE ONE HAND, AND THE ALLIANCE OF MOTION PICTURE AND
TELEVISION PRODUCERS, ON BEHALF OF THE COMPANIES LISTED ON EXHIBIT
"A" ATTACHED HERETO AND THOSE PRODUCERS WHICH HAVE EFFECTIVELY
CONSENTED TO BE A PART OF THE SINGLE MULTI-EMPLOYER BARGAINING UNIT,
ON THE OTHER HAND. THIS MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT IS EFFECTIVE AS OF
AUGUST 1, 2024, OR THE FIRST SUNDAY FOLLOWING THE BUSINESS DAY ON
WHICH THE AMPTP RECEIVES NOTICE OF RATIFICATION, WHICHEVER IS LATER.

PROVISIONS

1.

This Local Memorandum of Agreement along with any attachments or exhibits reflects
the understanding reached between the parties and will be supplemented and/or modified
to include appropriate changes and effective dates contained in the executed
2024 General Memorandum of Agreement.

2.

All agreements are subject to the terms and conditions of any agreed-upon extensions.

3.

All agreements are subject to final agreement in General Negotiations and to ratification
and execution by the parties.

4.

All issues not resolved herein are withdrawn.

Seniority
Modify Paragraph 68(a)(1) of the Local 705 Agreement to provide as follows:
“68.

Seniority
“(a)

Maintenance of Industry Experience Roster
***

“(1) The Industry Experience Roster shall consist of a single seniority group,
identified as Industry Group 1, containing the following classifications:
“(i)

Journeyperson Classification

“The Journeyperson classification shall consist of all
persons whose names appeared on the Industry Experience Roster in the Journeyperson
classification on July 31, 2024 2021, all persons who have been transferred to the
Journeyperson classification from the Entry Level classification as provided in
subparagraph (a)(2) below and all persons who worked in any of the job classifications
covered by this Agreement at least two hundred forty (240) pensionable hours during the
term of the 1979 Agreement between Producer and Local 705.
“(ii)

Entry Level Classification

“(A) The Entry Level classification shall be established
effective as of November 11, 1982 and shall include all persons on said Industry
Experience Roster not in the Journeyperson classification, other than those in the
Costume Maker classifications, who have worked in any of the job classifications
covered under this Agreement at least thirty (30) workdays collectively with one or more
Producers within a period of two (2) years three hundred sixty-five (365) calendar days
immediately preceding the date such employee applies for placement on the Roster.
“(B) In addition, any person who has one hundred (100)
days of work experience as provided in this subparagraph (B) within the three (3) years
immediately preceding the date such person applies for placement on the Industry
Experience Roster shall be entitled to apply for placement in the Entry Level
classification thereon. All such work experience must be of the type performed by job
classifications under this Agreement, other than Costume Maker classifications, and must
be performed in connection with motion pictures of the type covered under the Local 705
Agreement or the Videotape Electronics Supplemental Basic Agreement. The work
experience required for placement under this subparagraph (B) may be satisfied by

employment with companies signatory to the IATSE Basic Agreement or the Videotape
Electronics Supplemental Basic Agreement and/or with non-signatory companies,
provided such work is performed within the United States and its territories.
“(C) In addition, any person who has two hundred (200) days of
work experience as provided in this subparagraph (C) within the three (3) years
immediately preceding the date such person applies for placement on the Industry
Experience Roster shall be entitled to apply for placement in the Entry Level
classification thereon. All such work experience must be work performed by
professional costumers in connection with professional dramatic plays, musicals or
operas with professional actors and/or singers (e.g., those plays, musicals or operas
performed at the Ahmanson Theatre, Mark Taper Forum or Dorothy Chandler Pavilion),
and must be of the type performed by job classifications under this Agreement, other than
Costume Maker classifications. The work experience required for placement under this
subparagraph (C) may be satisfied by employment with companies signatory to the
IATSE Basic Agreement or the Videotape Electronics Supplemental Basic Agreement
and/or with non-signatory companies, provided such work is performed within the United
States and its territories.
“Any person making application to be placed on the Industry Experience
Roster must perfect the application no later than one (1) year following the date of the
last work day to be considered as qualifying experience.
“(2)

Advancement to Journeyperson Classification

“Any person in the Entry Level classification is eligible to be
advanced to the Journeyperson classification when such person has been placed in the
single seniority group for a period of over one (1) year and has acquired two hundred
(200) actual workdays since the date of placement.
“(3)

Eligibility for Employment as Costumer Keyperson

“After completing the requirements to be placed in the
Journeyperson classification, a Costumer with three (3) years Journeyperson experience
who has acquired at least two hundred (200) actual workdays as a Journeyperson
Costumer may be employed as a Costumer Keyperson (Occ. Code No. 2303).
“(4) By way of clarification, the parties agree that the Entry Level
classification is for purposes of determining the rate of pay only.”
