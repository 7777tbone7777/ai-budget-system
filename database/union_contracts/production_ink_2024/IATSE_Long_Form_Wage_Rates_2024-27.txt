IATSE West Coast Studio Local Long-Form Wage Rate Schedules
Wage Schedule Description
#
1 Long Form Sideletter Minimum Wage Schedule - rates reduced by 10%
• Long-form television motion pictures (including movies-of-the-week and two (2) hour pilots
for which no commitment for a series exists at the time of the pilot order) made for network
prime time and pay television for which principal photography commences on or after 8/4/2024
• One-time HB SVOD, HB AVOD or HB FAST Channel Program 66+ minutes in length, other
than 96+ minute Programs budgeted at ≥ $37,212,698, for which principal photography
commences on or after 8/4/2024
2 Long Form Sideletter Minimum Wage Schedule - rates reduced by 12.5%
• Long-form motion pictures, mini-series, pilots and 1st season of episodic series made for
basic cable for which principal photography of the program, first part of the mini-series, pilot or
first episode of the season commences on or after 8/4/2024
• One-time Mid-Budget SVOD, Mid-Budget AVOD or Mid-Budget FAST Channel Program for
which principal photography commences on or after 8/4/2024
• Mid-Budget SVOD, Mid-Budget AVOD or Mid-Budget FAST Channel Series for which
principal photography of the first episode of the season, or first part in the case of a miniseries, commences on or after 8/4/2024
• One-time Low-Budget SVOD, Low-Budget AVOD or Low-Budget FAST Channel Program for
which principal photography commences on or after 8/4/2024
• Low-Budget SVOD, Low-Budget AVOD or Low-Budget FAST Channel Series for which
principal photography of the first episode of the season, or first part in the case of a miniseries, commences on or after 8/4/2024
3 Long Form Sideletter Minimum Wage Schedule - general wage increases
• Long-form television motion pictures (including movies-of-the-week, mini-series and two (2)
hour pilots for which no commitment for a series exists at the time of the pilot order) made for
network prime time and pay television for which principal photography commences prior to
8/4/2024
• Long-form motion pictures made for videocassette/DVD market budgeted at ≤ $9,000,000
• Made-for-home video productions
• Long-form motion pictures, mini-series, pilots and 1st season of episodic series made for
basic cable for which principal photography of the program, first part of the mini-series, pilot or
first episode of the season commences prior to 8/4/2024
• 66+ minute HB SVOD mini-series budgeted at ≥ $3,000,000 but < $4,000,000 made for a
platform with ≥ 20M subscribers and 66+ minute HB SVOD mini-series budgeted at
≥ $3,000,000 but < $6,180,000 made for a platform with < 20M subscribers for which principal
photography of the first part commences prior to 8/4/2024
• One-time HB SVOD Program 66-84 minutes in length for which principal photography
commences prior to 8/4/2024
• One-time HB SVOD Program 85+ minutes in length budgeted at < $20,600,000 for which
principal photography commences prior to 8/4/2024
• One-time Mid-Budget SVOD Program for which principal photography commences prior to
8/4/2024
• Mid-Budget SVOD Series for which principal photography of the first episode of the season,
or first part in the case of a mini-series, commences prior to 8/4/2024
4 Long Form Sideletter Minimum Wage Schedule - "2x Back" Rates
• One-time Low-Budget SVOD Program for which principal photography commences prior to
8/4/2024
• Low-Budget SVOD Series for which principal photography of the first episode of the season,
or first part in the case of a mini-series, commences prior to 8/4/2024
